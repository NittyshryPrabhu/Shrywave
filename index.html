About Me

Hi, I'm Nittyshry Prabhu

I'm Nittyshry Prabhu, a passionate Writer , storyteller, actor, filmmaker, and driven by the belief that art has the power to inspire, connect, and leave a lasting impact. My journey began with a deep love for writing, which gradually expanded into filmmaking, acting.

I'm pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering, where I explored both academics and creativity together. My education gives me a strong foundation, while my passion helpes me evolve as a creative professional.

Over the years, I have honed my skills in storytelling, screenwriting, acting, and music composition, constantly experimenting with different forms of art. My vision is to create meaningful films, powerful performances, and soulful music that deeply connect with audiences.

I am building my career as a multifaceted creative artist, focusing on projects that combine cinematic storytelling and music to deliver impactful experiences. I believe in learning through collaboration and working with people who share the same passion for creativity and innovation.
